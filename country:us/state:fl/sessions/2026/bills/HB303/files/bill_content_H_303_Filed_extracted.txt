Section 1:
HB 303 2026
1 A bill to be entitled
2 An act relating to human trafficking training for
3 nursing students; amending s. 464.019, F.S.; revising
4 nursing education program requirements to include a
5 course on human trafficking prevention; requiring such
6 course to meet specified requirements; requiring
7 students to complete the course before graduation;
8 requiring the Board of Nursing, in coordination with
9 the Department of Health, to oversee implementation
10 and enforcement of the new education requirements;
11 requiring the board and the department to consult with
12 certain entities to ensure the course curriculum
13 remains consistent with certain criteria; providing
14 applicability; providing an effective date.
15
16 WHEREAS, the Legislature finds that human trafficking is a
17 critical public health crisis and safety issue affecting
18 thousands of individuals across Florida, and
19 WHEREAS, nurses play a key role in identifying and
20 assisting victims of human trafficking, as nurses are often
21 among the first and the last health care providers to interact
22 with individuals at risk, and
23 WHEREAS, the state has already taken steps to educate
24 licensed nurses on human trafficking through chapter 2017-23,
25 Laws of Florida, which requires continuing education on human

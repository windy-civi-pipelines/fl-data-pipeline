Section 1:
HB 197 2026
1 A bill to be entitled
2 An act relating to employment eligibility; amending s.
3 448.095, F.S.; requiring that all private employers,
4 rather than only those employing more than a specified
5 number of employees, use the E-Verify system to verify
6 a new employee's employment eligibility beginning on a
7 specified date; providing an effective date.
8
9 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Florida:
10
11 Section 1. Paragraph (b) of subsection (2) of section
12 448.095, Florida Statutes, is amended to read:
13 448.095 Employment eligibility.—
14 (2) EMPLOYMENT VERIFICATION.—
15 (b)1. A public agency shall use the E-Verify system to
16 verify a new employee's employment eligibility as required under
17 paragraph (a).
18 2. Beginning on July 1, 2026 2023, a private employer with
19 25 or more employees shall use the E-Verify system to verify a
20 new employee's employment eligibility as required under
21 paragraph (a).
22 3. Each employer required to use the E-Verify system under
23 this paragraph must certify on its first return each calendar
24 year to the tax service provider that it is in compliance with
25 this section when making contributions to or reimbursing the

    2         An act relating to a review under the Open Government
    3         Sunset Review Act; amending s. 319.1414, F.S., which
    4         provides an exemption from public records requirements
    5         for information received by the Department of Highway
    6         Safety and Motor Vehicles as a result of an
    7         investigation or examination of a department
    8         authorized private rebuilt inspection provider;
    9         removing the scheduled repeal of the exemption;
   10         amending ss. 319.25, 320.861, and 322.71, F.S., which
   11         provide exemptions from public records requirements
   12         for information received by the department as a result
   13         of an investigation or examination of a person
   14         suspected of having violated certain laws, rules, or
   15         orders; removing the scheduled repeal of such
   16         exemptions; providing an effective date.

1 A bill to be entitled
2 An act relating to home inspectors; amending s.
3 468.8313, F.S.; requiring that a home inspector
4 examination meet specified requirements certified by
5 the Department of Business and Professional
6 Regulation; amending s. 468.8322, F.S.; requiring home
7 inspectors to maintain an errors and omissions
8 insurance policy; providing an effective date.
9
10 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Florida:
11
12 Section 1. Subsection (4) of section 468.8313, Florida
13 Statutes, is amended to read:
14 468.8313 Examinations.—
15 (4) The department may review and approve national
16 examinations and state examinations that otherwise meet the
17 generally accepted testing standards criteria of national
18 examinations that the department has certified under s.
19 455.217(1)(d) by a nationally recognized entity that offers
20 programs or sets standards that ensure competence as a home
21 inspector.

23 to read:
24 468.8322 Insurance.—A home inspector shall maintain a

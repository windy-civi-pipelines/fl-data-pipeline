Section 1:
HB 429 2026
1 A bill to be entitled
2 An act relating to criteria for determining criminal
3 gang membership; amending s. 874.03, F.S.; revising
4 the definition of the term "criminal gang member";
5 providing an effective date.
6
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Florida:
8
9 Section 1. Subsection (3) of section 874.03, Florida
10 Statutes, is amended to read:
11 874.03 Definitions.—As used in this chapter:
12 (3) "Criminal gang member" is a person who:
13 (a) Admits, in person or on social media, to criminal gang
14 membership; or
15 (b) Meets two or more of the following criteria:
16 (a) Admits to criminal gang membership.
17 1.(b) Is identified as a criminal gang member by a parent,
18 or guardian, or spouse who resides with the person.
19 2.(c) Is identified as a criminal gang member by a
20 documented reliable informant.
21 3.(d) Adopts the style of dress of a criminal gang.
22 4.(e) Adopts the use of a hand sign identified as used by
23 a criminal gang.
24 5.(f) Has a tattoo identified as used by a criminal gang.
25 6.(g) Associates with one or more known criminal gang

Section 1:
HB 111 2026
1 A bill to be entitled
2 An act relating to the Antisemitism Task Force;
3 creating s. 16.571, F.S.; creating the Antisemitism
4 Task Force within the Office of Civil Rights of the
5 Department of Legal Affairs for a specified purpose;
6 requiring the department to provide administrative and
7 staff support to the task force; providing for
8 appointment and terms of members; providing for per
9 diem and travel expenses; providing membership and
10 duties of the task force; requiring a report to the
11 Governor and the Legislature by a specified date;
12 providing for future review and repeal of the task
13 force; providing an effective date.
14
15 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Florida:
16
17 Section 1. Section 16.571, Florida Statutes, is created to
18 read:
19 16.571 Antisemitism Task Force.—
20 (1) The Antisemitism Task Force, a task force as defined
21 in s. 20.03(5), is created adjunct to the Office of Civil Rights
22 within the Department of Legal Affairs for the express purpose
23 of combating antisemitism in this state. Except as otherwise
24 provided in this section, the task force shall operate in a
25 manner consistent with s. 20.052. The department shall provide

28 (2)(a) The task force is composed of the following

43 Leader of the Senate.
44 4. One member of the House of Representatives appointed by

46 5. Two members appointed by the Governor, one of whom must
47 have legal expertise in civil rights law.
48 6. The Attorney General or his or her designee.
49 7. The Commissioner of Education or his or her designee.
50 8. The chair of the Florida Commission on Human Relations

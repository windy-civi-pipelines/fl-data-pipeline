Section 1:
HB 7007 2026
1 A bill to be entitled
2 An act relating to a review under the Open Government
3 Sunset Review Act; amending s. 288.075, F.S., which
4 provides an exemption from public record requirements
5 for certain information held by an economic
6 development agency; removing the scheduled repeal of
7 the exemption; providing an effective date.
8
9 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Florida:
10
11 Section 1. Paragraph (c) of subsection (7) of section
12 288.075, Florida Statutes, is amended to read:
13 288.075 Confidentiality of records.—
14 (7) LOAN PROGRAMS.—
15 (c) This subsection is subject to the Open Government
16 Sunset Review Act in accordance with s. 119.15 and shall stand
17 repealed on October 2, 2026, unless reviewed and saved from
18 repeal through reenactment by the Legislature.
19 Section 2. This act shall take effect upon becoming a law.

Section 1:
HB 271 2026
1 A bill to be entitled
2 An act relating to foreign and alien bail bond
3 insurers; amending s. 624.4094, F.S.; providing duties
4 of certain foreign and alien bail bond insurers
5 relating to reporting bail bond premiums to the Office
6 of Insurance Regulation, keeping records of
7 considerations paid for bail bonds written by the
8 insurers, and disclosing certain information in the
9 financial statements filed with the office; providing
10 an effective date.
11
12 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Florida:
13
14 Section 1. Section 624.4094, Florida Statutes, is amended
15 to read:
16 624.4094 Bail bond premiums.—
17 (1) The Legislature finds that a significant portion of
18 bail bond premiums is retained by the licensed bail bond agents
19 or appointed managing general agents. For purposes of reporting
20 in financial statements required to be filed with the office
21 pursuant to s. 624.424, direct written premiums for bail bonds
22 by a domestic, foreign, or alien insurer doing business in this
23 state shall be reported net of any amounts retained by licensed
24 bail bond agents or appointed managing general agents. However,
25 in no case shall the direct written premiums for bail bonds be

27 agent for all bail bonds written by the agent. This subsection
28 also applies to any determination of compliance with s.

30 (2) Premiums assumed by a domestic, foreign, or alien
31 insurer shall be reported consistent with subsections (1) and

33 (3) Each domestic, foreign, or alien bail bond insurer

37 shall disclose the following information in the notes to the
38 financial statement in the insurer's annual statement filed with
39 the office.
40 (a) The gross bail bond premiums written in each state by

42 (b) The amount of premium taxes incurred by the company in
43 each state.
44 (c) Total consideration withheld by agents and not
45 reported as an expense by the insurer in financial statements
46 filed with the office.
47 (d) The amount of bail bond premium included on the surety
48 line of the annual statement filed with the office.
49 Section 2. This act shall take effect July 1, 2026.

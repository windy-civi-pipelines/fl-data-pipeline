Section 1:
HB 367 2026
1 A bill to be entitled
2 An act relating to cost-sharing requirements for
3 insulin and diabetes supplies; creating s. 627.42398,
4 F.S.; defining the term "cost-sharing requirement";
5 providing applicability; prohibiting health insurance
6 policies from imposing cost-sharing requirements that
7 exceed a specified amount for 30 days of insulin and
8 diabetes supplies; providing an effective date.
9
10 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Florida:
11
12 Section 1. Section 627.42398, Florida Statutes, is created
13 to read:
14 627.42398 Caps on cost-sharing requirements for insulin
15 and diabetes supplies.—
16 (1) As used in this section, the term "cost-sharing
17 requirement" means an insured's deductible, coinsurance,
18 copayment, or similar out-of-pocket expense.
19 (2) This section applies to all health insurance policies
20 delivered, issued, renewed, or amended in this state on or after
21 January 1, 2027, including policies provided by group, blanket,
22 or franchise health insurers; prepaid limited health service
23 organizations and discount plan organizations; and health
24 maintenance organizations.
25 (3) A health insurance policy may not impose a cost-

27 insulin for any insured, regardless of the quantity or type of
28 insulin prescribed.
29 (4) A health insurance policy may not impose a cost-

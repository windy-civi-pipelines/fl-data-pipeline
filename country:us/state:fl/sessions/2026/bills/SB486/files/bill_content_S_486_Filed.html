    1                        A bill to be entitled                      
    2         An act relating to discrimination in education;
    3         amending s. 1001.42, F.S.; requiring each district
    4         school board to designate a Title VI coordinator;
    5         specifying requirements for and duties of the
    6         coordinator; authorizing a person alleging
    7         discrimination to file a complaint with the district
    8         school superintendent or the Title VI coordinator;
    9         authorizing a parent or guardian to submit a complaint
   10         on behalf of a minor child; requiring the State Board
   11         of Education to adopt rules; creating s. 1004.041,
   12         F.S.; requiring each Florida College System
   13         institution and state university to designate a Title
   14         VI coordinator; specifying requirements for and duties
   15         of the coordinator; authorizing a person alleging
   16         discrimination to file a complaint with the dean of
   17         students or the Title VI coordinator; authorizing a
   18         parent or guardian to submit a complaint on behalf of
   19         a minor child; requiring the State Board of Education
   20         and Board of Governors to adopt rules and regulations,
   21         respectively; providing an effective date.

Section 1:
HB 463 2026
1 A bill to be entitled
2 An act relating to certificate of title application
3 requirements; amending s. 319.23, F.S.; including
4 certain proof of identification and residency in
5 requirements for certificate of title applications;
6 providing an effective date.
7
8 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Florida:
9
10 Section 1. Paragraph (d) is added to subsection (3) of
11 section 319.23, Florida Statutes, to read:
12 319.23 Application for, and issuance of, certificate of
13 title.—
14 (3) If a certificate of title has not previously been
15 issued for a motor vehicle or mobile home in this state, the
16 application, unless otherwise provided for in this chapter,
17 shall be accompanied by a proper bill of sale or sworn statement
18 of ownership, or a duly certified copy thereof, or by a
19 certificate of title, bill of sale, or other evidence of
20 ownership required by the law of the state or county from which
21 the motor vehicle or mobile home was brought into this state.
22 The application shall also be accompanied by:
23 (d)1. The applicant's proof of identity satisfactory to
24 the department. Such proof must include one of the following
25 documents issued to the applicant:

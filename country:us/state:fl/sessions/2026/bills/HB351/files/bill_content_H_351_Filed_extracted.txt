Section 1:
HB 351 2026
1 A bill to be entitled
2 An act relating to concurrent legislative jurisdiction
3 over United States military installations; creating s.
4 250.031, F.S.; providing that this state has
5 concurrent legislative jurisdiction with the United
6 States over United States military installations
7 located within the boundaries of this state; providing
8 procedures and requirements therefor; providing an
9 effective date.
10
11 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Florida:
12
13 Section 1. Section 250.031, Florida Statutes, is created
14 to read:
15 250.031 United States military installations; concurrent
16 legislative jurisdiction.—
17 (1) This state accepts the relinquishment of exclusive
18 legislative jurisdiction from the United States in accordance
19 with this section.
20 (2) This state has concurrent legislative jurisdiction
21 with the United States over any United States military
22 installation under the control of the United States which is
23 located within the boundaries of this state.
24 (3) The concurrent legislative jurisdiction over a United
25 States military installation under this section is effective

67 government, or special district may enter a reciprocal agreement
68 with a United States agency to designate duties related to the

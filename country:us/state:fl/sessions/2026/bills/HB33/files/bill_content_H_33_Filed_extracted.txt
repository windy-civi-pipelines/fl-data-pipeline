Section 1:
HB 33 2026
1 A bill to be entitled
2 An act relating to transportation facility
3 designations; providing an honorary designation of a
4 certain transportation facility in a specified county;
5 directing the Department of Transportation to erect
6 suitable markers; providing an effective date.
7
8 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Florida:
9
10 Section 1. Charlie Kirk Memorial Avenue designated;
11 Department of Transportation to erect suitable markers.—
12 (1) That portion of S.R. 985/S.W. 107th Avenue between
13 S.W. 24th Street and S.R. 90/S.W. 8th Street in Miami-Dade
14 County is designated as "Charlie Kirk Memorial Avenue."
15 (2) The Department of Transportation is directed to erect
16 suitable markers designating Charlie Kirk Memorial Avenue as
17 described in subsection (1).
18 Section 2. This act shall take effect July 1, 2026.

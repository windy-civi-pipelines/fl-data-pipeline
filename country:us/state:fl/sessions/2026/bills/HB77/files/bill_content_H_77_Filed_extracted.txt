Section 1:
HB 77 2026
1 A bill to be entitled
2 An act relating to public postsecondary safety
3 policies and procedures; creating s. 1004.0983, F.S.;
4 requiring state universities and Florida College
5 System institutions to adopt and implement specified
6 policies and procedures relating to safety; providing
7 requirements for the policies and procedures;
8 requiring the universities and institutions to
9 annually review and update the policies and
10 procedures; authorizing the Board of Governors and the
11 State Board of Education to adopt regulations and
12 rules, respectively; providing an effective date.
13
14 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Florida:
15
16 Section 1. Section 1004.0983, Florida Statutes, is created
17 to read:
18 1004.0983 Public postsecondary safety policies and
19 procedures.—
20 (1) Each state university and Florida College System
21 institution shall adopt and implement written policies and
22 procedures to protect the physical safety of its students,
23 faculty, staff, and visitors in campus facilities, classrooms,
24 and other institutional settings from:
25 (a) Physical threats.

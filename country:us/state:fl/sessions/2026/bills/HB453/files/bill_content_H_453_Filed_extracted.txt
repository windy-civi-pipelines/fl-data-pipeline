Section 1:
HB 453 2026
1 A bill to be entitled
2 An act relating to requirements for a standard high
3 school diploma; amending s. 1003.4282, F.S.; providing
4 that students with disabilities may participate in the
5 Special Olympics to meet the physical education
6 requirement for a standard high school diploma;
7 providing an effective date.
8
9 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Florida:
10
11 Section 1. Paragraph (b) of subsection (8) of section
12 1003.4282, Florida Statutes, is amended to read:
13 1003.4282 Requirements for a standard high school
14 diploma.—
15 (8) STUDENTS WITH DISABILITIES.—Beginning with students
16 entering grade 9 in the 2014-2015 school year, this subsection
17 applies to a student with a disability.
18 (b) The following options, in addition to the other
19 options specified in this section, may be used to satisfy the
20 standard high school diploma requirements, as specified in the
21 student's individual education plan:
22 1. For a student with a disability for whom the IEP team
23 has determined that the Florida Alternate Assessment is the most
24 appropriate measure of the student's skills:
25 a. A combination of course substitutions, assessments,

Section 1:
HB 4017 2026
1 A bill to be entitled
2 An act relating to Nassau County; repealing chapters
3 61-2525 and 69-1331, Laws of Florida, relating to the
4 Nassau County Recreation and Water Conservation and
5 Control Districts; abolishing the district;
6 transferring all assets and liabilities of the
7 districts to the Board of County Commissioners of
8 Nassau County; providing an effective date.
9
10 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Florida:
11
12 Section 1. Chapters 61-2525 and 69-1331, Laws of Florida,
13 are repealed.
14 Section 2. The Nassau County Recreation and Water
15 Conservation and Control Districts are abolished. All assets and
16 liabilities of the districts are transferred to the Board of
17 County Commissioners of Nassau County.
18 Section 3. This act shall take effect upon becoming a law.
